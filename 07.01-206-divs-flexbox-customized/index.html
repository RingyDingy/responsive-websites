Three Deadlands characters described in one paragraph each
Making a character in The Weird West is usually as easy as picking your favorite trope and expanding it, but don't forget to add an interesting backstory hook or two! Below are three examples of characters to get your creative juices flowing:
A hard-faced man with a black hat and a weathered revolver, The Stranger wandered into town the same time the troubles started. Is he the cause of the troubles? Is he pursued by some unnatural force? Or could he be the town's salvation?
Pastor Warhol is on his third church in three years, the previous two having both been burned to the ground- by who or what, nobody knows except Warhol himself, and even during a rare sober spell, he won't talk about his former flocks.
"Dandy" Lawrence makes his living with a deck of cards. He travels from town to town, playing poker wherever he stops. He never stays in one place long, because though he's never been caught cheating, his uncanny luck quickly wears out his welcome.
